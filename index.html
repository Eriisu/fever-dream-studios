About Us


Recognised as a global leader in interactive and digital entertainment, Fever Dream Studios (FDS) is responsible for multiple popular brands and families of products. Fever Dream Studios have delivered innovation to the market since the launch of their first production in England in 2007.

Headquartered in London, England, FDS is an independently owned business, and has global functions in California, Dubai and Tokyo.
